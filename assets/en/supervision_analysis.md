## 1. Technical Note: Mastering Standard
**Reference:** Spectral Analysis Protocol (SuperVision Module / Spectrogram)

### 2. Frequency Signature
The spectrogram analysis reveals a balanced sound architecture, compliant with the rigorous requirements of the **Foliotype Protocol**:

![Spectrogram Analysis](../assets/mastering/audio-analysis/spectrogram.png)

* **Foundations (Sub/Bass):** Presence of a solid and constant base in the low frequencies.
* **Midrange Definition:** Marked clarity in the 1 kHz to 3 kHz range, guaranteeing the intelligibility of the **Hermes** voice.
* **Harmonic Extension:** Linear extension into high frequencies for "brilliance".

### 3. Dynamics and Density
The visual rendering identifies rigorous dynamic control, certified by our measurement tools:

![Phase Correlation](../assets/mastering/audio-analysis/phase_correlation.png)

* **Transient Management:** Clear alternation between energy peaks and release phases.
* **Stereophonic Consistency:** Symmetry between L/R channels confirms stable phase correlation.

### 4. Validation Criteria for Final Master
Under the **Foliotype** standard, every export must present:
1.  An absence of spectral "holes".
2.  A spectral balance where no narrow resonance dominates aggressively.
3.  Low-end management free of rumble (cleaning below 30-40 Hz).
